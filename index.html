Our story

It started with a farmer who wondered if he could do more with less.

Davaus began in Convoy, Ohio, when a local farmer looked at his Kinze planter and asked a deceptively simple question. The patented BackerPlate was the answer, and then a vacuum-meter version followed. SeedRight became real product, not a side project.

In late 2016, Peridot Inc., a Hoagland, Indiana prototyping and manufacturing company with twenty years of engineering chops, acquired the SeedRight business. Davaus was born: a product company with an engineering team, a dealer network across the U.S., and a manufacturer it owns.

Innovative solutions to agriculture, rooted in the science of agronomy, built to improve the grower's bottom line.
